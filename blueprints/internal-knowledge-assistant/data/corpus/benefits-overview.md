# Benefits Overview

The company offers medical, dental, and vision insurance, with the company covering 80 percent
of the premium for employees and 60 percent for dependents. Coverage begins on the first of the
month following your start date.

You may enroll or change your benefits during your new-hire window (first 30 days) or during
annual open enrollment in November. Outside those windows, you can only change benefits after a
qualifying life event such as marriage, birth, or loss of other coverage; you have 30 days from
the event to make changes.

The company matches 401(k) contributions dollar for dollar up to 4 percent of your salary.
Matching contributions vest immediately. You can change your contribution rate at any time
through the benefits portal.

Additional benefits include an annual wellness stipend, an employee assistance program, and
paid parental leave. See the benefits portal for current details and plan documents.
