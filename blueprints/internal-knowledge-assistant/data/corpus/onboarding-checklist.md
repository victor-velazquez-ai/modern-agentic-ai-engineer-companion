# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

Welcome aboard. Your first week is about getting set up and meeting your team. Use this
checklist to make sure nothing is missed.

Day one: pick up your laptop and badge from IT, enable MFA, sign in to SSO, and join your
team's communication channels. Your manager will share a 30-60-90 day plan and introduce you
to your onboarding buddy.

Week one: complete security onboarding, set up the VPN, submit your home-office stipend if you
are hybrid or remote, and book a 1:1 with your manager. Read your team's runbook and the
engineering handbook if you are in a technical role.

By day 30: complete required compliance training, finish any role-specific setup, and confirm
your benefits enrollment with HR. If anything is blocking you, file a ticket or ask your buddy
rather than staying stuck.
