# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a workplace that is respectful, inclusive, and free of harassment. Treat
colleagues, customers, and partners with professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, and
retaliation are prohibited and are grounds for disciplinary action up to termination.

Avoid conflicts of interest. Disclose any outside activity, investment, or relationship that
could influence — or appear to influence — your work decisions. When in doubt, disclose to your
manager or to the ethics office.

Protect company and customer information. Only access data you need for your job, and only share
it with people authorized to see it. Report suspected violations of this code through your
manager, HR, or the anonymous ethics hotline. Reports made in good faith will not result in
retaliation.

This code applies to everyone, including contractors and the leadership team.
