# Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees may be reimbursed for reasonable business expenses incurred while performing
their job. Common reimbursable expenses include travel, lodging, client meals, software
subscriptions used for work, and home-office equipment pre-approved by your manager.

Submit expenses through the finance portal within 30 days of the expense date. Each expense
must include an itemized receipt. Expenses submitted more than 60 days after the expense date
may be denied. Reimbursements are paid with the next regular payroll run after approval.

Meals during travel are reimbursed up to a daily limit of $75 in the United States and the
local equivalent elsewhere. Alcohol is not reimbursable. Ride-share and public transit to and
from the airport are reimbursable; parking is reimbursable up to $25 per day.

Expenses over $500 require manager approval before they are incurred, not after.
