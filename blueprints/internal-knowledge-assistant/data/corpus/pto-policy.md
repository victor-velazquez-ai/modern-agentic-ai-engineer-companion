# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Full-time employees accrue paid time off at a rate of 1.5 days per month, for a total
of 18 days per year. PTO begins accruing on your first day and is available to use once
it has accrued. You do not need to wait for a probationary period to use PTO.

To request time off, submit a request in the HR portal at least two weeks in advance for
any absence longer than three consecutive days. Your manager approves PTO requests; if
your manager is unavailable, their manager may approve in their place.

Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year. Any balance
above 5 days is forfeited on January 1. The company does not pay out unused PTO except where
required by local law at the time of separation.

Sick leave is tracked separately from PTO and does not count against your PTO balance.
