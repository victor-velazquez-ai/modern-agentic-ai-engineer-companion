# Remote Work and Hybrid Schedule

The company operates on a hybrid model. Most teams are expected in the office on Tuesday and
Wednesday, with the remaining days flexible. Fully remote arrangements are available for some
roles and require VP approval and a signed remote-work agreement.

When working remotely you are responsible for a reliable internet connection and a quiet,
secure workspace. Do not work from public networks without the company VPN. Confidential
material must never be visible to anyone outside the company, including household members.

The company provides a one-time $500 home-office stipend for remote and hybrid employees to
purchase a desk, chair, or monitor. Submit the stipend through the expense portal; it does not
count against your normal expense limits.

Core collaboration hours are 10am to 3pm in your team's primary time zone. Keep your calendar
and status up to date so colleagues know when to reach you.
