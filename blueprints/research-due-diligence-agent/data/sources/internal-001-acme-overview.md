# Internal memo — Acme Vector DB Inc. (target company overview)

Source: data-room / internal due-diligence memo
Doc type: internal
Date: 2025-Q1

Acme Vector DB Inc. sells a managed vector database for retrieval-augmented
generation (RAG) workloads. The company was founded in 2021 and is headquartered
in Austin, Texas. Acme's product offers hybrid search (dense embeddings fused with
keyword BM25) and a serverless tier billed per stored vector and per query.

Acme reports roughly 240 paying customers as of the end of 2024, concentrated in
mid-market SaaS and two large financial-services accounts. Annual recurring revenue
(ARR) is approximately 18 million US dollars, up from 7 million the prior year, a
year-over-year growth rate near 157 percent. Gross margin is reported at 71 percent.

The leadership team is led by CEO Dana Ruiz, formerly a staff engineer on a large
search team, and CTO Priya Nair, who previously built the indexing layer at a
well-known analytics vendor. Headcount is 64, of which 38 are in engineering.
