# Industry brief — vector database market size and growth

Source: stubbed offline "web search" (mock analyst brief)
Doc type: web
Date: 2025

The vector database market is an emerging category created by the rise of retrieval
-augmented generation and semantic search. Independent analyst estimates put the 2024
market size at roughly 2.2 billion US dollars in annual spend across managed services
and self-hosted deployments. Most estimates project a compound annual growth rate
(CAGR) between 22 and 26 percent through 2030, which would place the market near 7 to
9 billion US dollars by the end of the decade.

Demand is driven by enterprise adoption of RAG: organizations storing their documents
as embeddings and retrieving them at query time to ground large-language-model
answers. Hybrid search (dense plus keyword) and reranking have become table-stakes
features that buyers now expect from any serious vendor.

Note: market-size figures vary widely between analysts and should be treated as
directional, not precise. This brief is illustrative data for the blueprint, not a
real market report.
