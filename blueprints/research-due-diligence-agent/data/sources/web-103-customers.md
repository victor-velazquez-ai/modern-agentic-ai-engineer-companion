# Industry brief — customer sentiment and references for Acme

Source: stubbed offline "web search" (mock reference-call digest)
Doc type: web
Date: 2025

Reference conversations with three Acme customers paint a consistent picture.
Customers praise retrieval quality: two independently reported that switching to
Acme's hybrid search improved answer relevance in their RAG applications compared with
a dense-only baseline they ran previously. Onboarding was described as fast, on the
order of days rather than weeks.

The recurring complaint is cost predictability. Because Acme bills per stored vector
and per query, customers with spiky query patterns found month-to-month bills hard to
forecast. One customer noted that at very large scale the serverless model became more
expensive than a reserved-capacity competitor.

Support quality was rated highly by all three references, who cited responsive
engineers and a public status page. None of the three reported a production outage in
the past year, though this is a small and self-selected sample.
