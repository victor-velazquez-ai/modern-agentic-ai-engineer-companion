# Industry brief — risks, security, and regulatory notes

Source: stubbed offline "web search" (mock risk scan)
Doc type: web
Date: 2025

Three categories of risk are worth flagging for a vector-database acquisition.

Platform risk: dedicated vector vendors face commoditization as incumbent databases
add vector indexes. A buyer should test whether Acme's hybrid-search quality is a
durable advantage or a temporary one.

Security and compliance: Acme holds SOC 2 Type II certification and offers data
residency in three regions. It does not yet hold FedRAMP authorization, which limits
sales to US federal agencies. Customer embeddings can encode sensitive source text,
so data-handling controls matter more here than for a generic cache.

Concentration risk: a meaningful share of revenue sits in two financial-services
accounts whose contracts renew in the second half of 2025. Loss of either would
materially dent ARR. This is the single most important diligence item to verify
against the data room before closing.
