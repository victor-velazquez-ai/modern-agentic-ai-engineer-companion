PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is between Provider and Buyer. Most terms track
Buyer's standard playbook; a small number deviate and warrant review.

1. Limitation of Liability
Each party's total aggregate liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the twelve (12) months
preceding the claim. Indirect and consequential damages are excluded.

2. Termination
Either party may terminate for convenience on 30 days' written notice, and for cause with a cure
period.

3. Confidentiality
Each party will protect the other's Confidential Information for three (3) years following
termination, with standard carve-outs.

4. Payment Terms
Buyer shall pay undisputed invoices Net 60 from the date of a valid invoice. This extended window is
longer than Buyer's standard Net 30 position.

5. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [Your State], venue in [Your County].

6. Warranty
Provider warrants the services will conform to the documentation for 90 days; the remedy is
re-performance.

7. IP Ownership
Each party retains its pre-existing intellectual property. Deliverable IP assigns to Buyer on full
payment.

8. Data Protection
A DPA governs personal data. Sub-processors require notice and breach notification is within 72
hours.
