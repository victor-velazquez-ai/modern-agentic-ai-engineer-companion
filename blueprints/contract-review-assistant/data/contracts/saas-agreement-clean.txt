MASTER SOFTWARE-AS-A-SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Software-as-a-Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Vendor and
Customer as of the Effective Date.

1. Limitation of Liability
Each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid in the
twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or
consequential damages.

2. Indemnification
Each party shall indemnify the other against third-party claims arising from its breach of this
Agreement, infringement of intellectual property, or gross negligence. The indemnities are mutual
and the indemnifying party controls the defense.

3. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on 30 days' written notice, and for cause
on 30 days' notice with a cure period. There is no automatic renewal without an opt-out window.

4. Confidentiality
Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information for a period of three (3) years
following termination, subject to standard carve-outs for information that is publicly known,
independently developed, or compelled by law.

5. Payment Terms
Customer shall pay all undisputed invoices Net 30 from the date of a valid invoice. Late amounts
accrue interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the legal maximum.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of [Your State], with venue in [Your County].

7. Warranty
Vendor warrants that the services will conform to the documentation for 90 days; the limited remedy
is re-performance of the non-conforming services.

8. IP Ownership
Each party retains its pre-existing intellectual property. IP in the deliverables assigns to
Customer on full payment; Vendor retains a license to its tools and know-how.

9. Data Protection
A Data Processing Addendum (DPA) governs the processing of personal data. Processing is limited to
documented instructions, sub-processors require prior notice, and Vendor will notify Customer of any
personal-data breach within 72 hours of becoming aware.
