# Two-factor authentication (2FA)

Turn on 2FA from Settings → Security. We support authenticator apps (TOTP) and SMS codes; an
authenticator app is more secure and works offline. When you enable 2FA we show you ten one-time
backup codes — save them somewhere safe.

If you've lost your device and your backup codes, support can start an identity-verified recovery
so you don't lose access. We can never read your current codes, and we will not disable 2FA on an
account without verifying you own it.
