# Deleting your account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone: it removes your data, cancels active
subscriptions, and revokes access for every member of your workspace. Because it is
irreversible, deletion is never performed automatically by support chat.

To request deletion, a workspace owner must confirm in writing from Settings → Danger Zone, or
ask support to escalate the request to our trust & safety team, who verify ownership before
anything is removed. GDPR erasure requests follow the same verified path.
