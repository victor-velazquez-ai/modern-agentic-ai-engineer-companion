# Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel any time from Settings → Billing → Cancel. Cancellation stops the next renewal;
you keep access to paid features until the end of the period you've already paid for, and we don't
charge you again after that.

Cancelling is not the same as deleting your account — your data and settings stay intact, and you
can resubscribe later without losing anything. If you cancel an annual plan, see the refund policy
for whether a partial refund applies.
