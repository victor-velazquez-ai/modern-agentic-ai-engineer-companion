# Shipping times and costs

Standard shipping is free and arrives in 5–7 business days within the contiguous US. Expedited
shipping (2 business days) is available at checkout for a flat fee. International orders ship in
7–14 business days; duties and import taxes are the recipient's responsibility.

Orders placed before 2pm on a business day ship the same day. We don't ship on weekends or public
holidays, so an order placed Friday afternoon begins processing the next business day.
