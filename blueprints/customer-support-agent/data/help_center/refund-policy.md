# Refund policy

We offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked. Refunds are issued to
the original payment method and take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement.

Refunds of $50 or less on an eligible order can be processed immediately by our support team.
Larger refunds, refunds outside the 30-day window, and refunds on annual plans require a
manager's review before the money is released. We do not process refunds initiated through a
payment-provider chargeback while a dispute is open.
