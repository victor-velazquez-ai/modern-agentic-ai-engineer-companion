# Plans and billing

We offer three subscription plans: Starter, Pro, and Enterprise. You can upgrade or downgrade
at any time from Settings → Billing. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the
remainder of your billing period; downgrades take effect at the start of your next cycle so you
keep the features you paid for until then.

Annual plans bill once a year at a discount. Changing between monthly and annual billing is
handled at renewal. Your invoices are available under Settings → Billing → Invoices.
