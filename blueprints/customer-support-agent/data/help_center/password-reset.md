# Resetting your password

If you're locked out or can't sign in, you can reset your password yourself. Open the sign-in
page and choose "Forgot password". Enter the email on your account and we'll send a reset link
that stays valid for 30 minutes. The link is single-use; request a new one if it expires.

If you don't see the email, check your spam folder and confirm you used the address on file.
Support can trigger a reset email for you, but we will never read or set your password for you.
