# Tracking your order

Every order has an id in the form ord_XXXX. You can see live fulfilment status and the estimated
delivery date under Account → Orders, or by giving support your order id. Statuses progress from
"processing" to "shipped" to "delivered".

Tracking information appears once an order ships. If your order has been in "processing" for more
than three business days, contact support and we'll look into it for you.
