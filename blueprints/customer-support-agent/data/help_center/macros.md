# Support macros (canned responses)

Macros are the short, reusable replies agents send for the most common asks. They are part of the
retrievable corpus so the agent can ground a deflection in an approved response, not improvise.

- greeting: "Thanks for reaching out — happy to help with that."
- password: "I can send a reset link to the email on your account; you'll be back in within a
  couple of minutes."
- order_eta: "Let me pull up that order and give you the latest status and delivery estimate."
- refund_small: "That's within our 30-day window and under our instant-refund limit, so I can
  process it right now."
- closing: "Is there anything else I can help you with today?"
