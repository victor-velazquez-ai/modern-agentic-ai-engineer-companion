# Refund Policy

We offer a full refund on any plan within **30 days** of purchase, no questions
asked. Refunds are issued to the original payment method and take 5–10 business
days to appear.

After 30 days, paid plans are non-refundable, but you can cancel at any time to
stop future charges (see `cancellation-policy.md`). Annual plans cancelled
mid-term are pro-rated only if the cancellation is due to a documented service
outage.

To request a refund, contact support with your order id. If the docs do not
resolve the question, support opens a ticket.
